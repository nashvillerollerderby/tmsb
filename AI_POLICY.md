# AI Usage Policy

This project **does not** include any LLM or generative AI features, and we commit to not introducing such features in the future.

This project is developed without the use of LLMs or generative AI tools.

We recognize that contributors may use a variety of workflows, including limited generative AI assistance (e.g., for code review or ideation). However, all contributions must be:

- substantially authored by humans
- fully understood by the contributor
- able to be explained and defended during review

We do not accept code, assets, or documentation that are wholly or substantially generated by generative AI.
